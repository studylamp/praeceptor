Using your tutor

Chat with your tutor the way you'd talk to a real one. Ask it to explain something, show a worked example, give you a practice problem, draw a graph, or quiz you. Stick to the subject you picked — if you need a different one, go back to Subjects and choose it.

Writing math questions

You can always just use words. Type “what is the square root of x plus one, all over two?” and the tutor will understand. It also writes the math back to you neatly, so you can check it understood your question.

When you want to be exact, here are the shortcuts most people use — starting simple and building up.

Not sure how to type something? Just describe it in words — that almost always works, and the tutor will show it back to you in proper math so you can confirm.
