Educational framing (optional)

Describe your family's educational or worldview framing and the tutor will honor it across every subject when presenting material on contested or worldview-sensitive topics. It shapes only how the tutor PRESENTS material; it does not change a subject's scope, age-appropriateness, or safety, and it never affects which messages are allowed. Spell out exactly what you want — for example, if you'd also like the tutor to cover how a topic is commonly taught elsewhere, say so here. Leave it blank for no framing. You can add a subject-specific note in each subject's editor. Takes effect on the student's next message.

Timezone

The zone used for daily-cap resets (caps roll over at this zone's midnight) and for the times shown on conversations. Message times are stored in UTC and converted for display. Leave it on "Server local time" to follow the machine's own clock. Takes effect right away.
